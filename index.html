Welcome to BloodConnect!

BloodConnect is a platform that connects blood donor and donee. Oh, it's free and non-profit!

Browse through requests

Feeling generous? Browse through blood requests and start donating. Need blood? Make a request and near users will be notified.

Give to those who need it the most

BloodConnect aims to find blood donors fast in cases of emergency and ensure healthy supply of blood in blood banks.
